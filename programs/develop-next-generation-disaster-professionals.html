University Partnership: R&D, Education & Engagement: Tapping the Potential of Universities to Develop the Next Generation of Disaster Professionals, Knowledge Creation, Campus Resilience Building and Community Service

 

Time: 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM, November 19

Location: UNH 110 - University Hall

A university educates and performs research. It is a community within itself as well as being part of a larger community that may extend some distance beyond its campus. This panel will explore these functions and dimensions of a university from the perspective of disaster planning and response, with the intent of producing initial generalized outcomes and recommendations that can be developed further in subsequent forums and venues.
